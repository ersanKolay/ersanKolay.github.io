What is NumConnect?
NumConnect is a challenging number puzzle game where you connect matching numbers to clear the grid. Your goal is to connect all pairs without crossing lines.
How do I connect numbers?
Tap on a number, then drag to connect it with its matching pair. The line cannot cross other lines or pass through occupied cells. All numbers must be connected to complete the puzzle.
